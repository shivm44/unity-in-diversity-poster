# 🌍 Unity in Diversity – Languages of India Poster

## 📌 Overview

This poster represents the theme **"Unity in Diversity"** by showcasing the rich linguistic diversity of India. It highlights how multiple languages, dialects, and cultural groups coexist harmoniously within one nation.

## 🎯 Theme

**Unity in Diversity – Languages of India**

India is home to:

* Over **19,500 dialects**
* **121 major languages**
* **22 official languages**
* **4 major language families**

Despite this diversity, the country remains united — reflecting the true spirit of *unity in diversity*.

## 🎨 Poster Highlights

* Visual map of major ethnolinguistic groups in India
* Key statistics about languages and dialects
* Representation of cultural diversity through people and attire
* Emphasis on unity across different linguistic backgrounds

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Canva (for design and layout)

## 🎓 Purpose

This poster was created for a **college competition** based on the theme *Unity in Diversity*. The goal is to spread awareness about India's linguistic richness and cultural harmony.
